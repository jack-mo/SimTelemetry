What is SimTelemetry?

SimTelemetry is a program to collect and display live telemetry data on a secondary computer monitor, laptop or any other device. We aim to provide detailed and honest information about your car and the competition on the virtual track. SimTelemetry also contains a telemetry logger with analyzer so you can review your own driving afterwards.

SimTelemetry is free of charge and completely open source. Find the latest build download at the downloads page. You can find the source code on GitHub.com. Developers can write their own plug-ins for new simulators, new data output streams and widgets.

Currently SimTelemetry is in a beta stage, but is in a stage of completion that it will be usable by most users.

Track map. Who is out there?

Never be surprised anymore what is up ahead and where your competitors drive on track. With the live trackmap you can see who is driving where in real time. Also a very useful tool for learning new tracks!

Maps are accurately generated on the fly and require no configuration work at all. No more unpacking game files and generating image files through a tool.

Car Telemetry. Never mess it up

Remember when you were pushing so close for the lead and your engine blew up? Or your race ended dramatically due to a brake failure.. Not anymore now, see how far consumables in your car are wearing and what their expected life span is.

Timing screens. Awesome2

See live timing of all cars like as if you were in the garage. But you're living it on the track! Have the same tools available to you as if you were a full professional racing team, all in favour for you race strategy.

Analyze Analyze Analyze

Optimize your driving style, car setup and strategy with the built in telemetry logging and viewing software. Over 200 car and driving parameters are logged at a high date rate to graph and visualize precisely what you did in that chicane right there.

In this example you see the Campus corners (turns 13 and 14) in sector 2 of Spa Franchorchamps. The graph shows the throttle and brake position for the corner.
